Blood Drive - Toll Gate High School

The Toll Gate Blood Drive is happens a couple times a year, and you can help! Here are some benefits to donating:

  • It can help save lives.
  • Hospitals always need blood.
  • It can help people injured in accidents, undergoing cancer treatment, and battling blood diseases, among other reasons.
